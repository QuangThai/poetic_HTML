About
Hue Poetic

Located in Hue City, in a building built from 2015, Poetic Hue Hotel is 2.8 km from Trang Tien Bridge and offers rooms with free WiFi. The property is 3.1 km from Hue Royal Museum and Dong Ba Market and 3.9 km from Chieu Ung Pagoda. The property has a 24-hour front desk, a business center and ticketing services.
